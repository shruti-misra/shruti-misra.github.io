Hi, I am Shruti Misra

Data Scientist, Aimpoint Digital

Hi, I'm a Data Scientist specializing in agentic and generative AI systems. I focus on building scalable, evaluation-driven solutions for enterprise applications, spanning the full AI lifecycle. My work includes designing and deploying Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) pipelines with LangGraph, as well as architecting Compound AI systems using LangChain—integrating capabilities like dynamic routing, text-to-SQL, geospatial reasoning, and external API orchestration. I’ve developed custom evaluation frameworks for benchmarking agentic workflows in both offline and online settings, enabling continuous performance monitoring and iterative optimization. I hold a Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering with a specialization in Data Science from the University of Washington, and I’m certified in both Databricks and Snowflake. ​
